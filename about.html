About Me+

Hello my name is Tessa, I am a Graphic Designer based in Bournemouth with 2 years of design experience within the industry, I am bilingual and I speak fluent Cantonese and English. Sleek and clean with a little boldness is my approach to design. I take joy in editorial design as I like producing images and pairing it subtle typography with the occasional ‘pop’ of colour that captures the attention of the observer to keep looking for more.

I have an interest for ‘fashion’ and I keep up to date with online fashion magazines and youtube videos. When I’m not arranging typefaces and picking out colour palettes you’ll find me playing my guitar and scrolling through designers Instagram. I am drawn to exploring and gaining any form of insight I can get in the creative industry, I hope to broaden my knowledge of graphic design and learn through new experiences.
